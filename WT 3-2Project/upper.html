Chin Up
CATEGORY:
Chest
DESCRIPTION:
Assume a support position, perform a push up, make sure to engage
your core while performing each repetition.
EQUIPMENT:
none (bodyweight exercise)
PRIMARY MUSCLE:
Pectoralis major
DEFAULT REPS:
10


Flat bench press

CATEGORY:
Chest
DESCRIPTION:
On you bench press rack of choice, set-up in flat configuration,
use a barbell to press up and down, make sure to have a spotter.
EQUIPMENT:
Barbell
PRIMARY MUSCLE:
Pectoralis major
DEFAULT REPS:
8


Dumbbell incline curl

CATEGORY:
Arms
DESCRIPTION:
With elbows back to sides, raise one dumbbell and rotate forearm until
forearm is vertical and palm faces shoulder.Lower to original position
and repeat with opposite arm. Continue to alternate between sides.
EQUIPMENT:
Dumbbell
PRIMARY MUSCLE:
Anterior deltoid
DEFAULT REPS:
8


Bent Over Rowing

CATEGORY:
Back
DESCRIPTION:
Grab the barbell with a wide grip (slightly more than shoulder wide)
and lean forward.Don't swing with your body and keep your arms next
to your body.
EQUIPMENT:
Barbell
PRIMARY MUSCLE:
Latissimus dorsi
DEFAULT REPS:
6
