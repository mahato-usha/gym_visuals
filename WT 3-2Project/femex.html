Chin Up

CATEGORY:
Legs
DESCRIPTION:
Start with legs slightly wider than shoulder width-Drop into a bodyweight squat
EQUIPMENT:
Barbell
PRIMARY MUSCLE:
Biceps femoris
DEFAULT REPS:
10

Side Leg lifts

CATEGORY:
abs
DESCRIPTION:
Lying on your side, check that your ankles, knees, hips, shoulders, and ears are aligned
Move your legs slightly in front (forming this "banana shape" helps balance and protects
your lower back).
EQUIPMENT:
none(bodyweight exercise
PRIMARY MUSCLE:
Obliquus externus abdominis
DEFAULT REPS:
16

Sit-up|Touch Toes|Pilates

CATEGORY:
abs
DESCRIPTION:
Get on a mat and lie on your back. Contract your abs, stretch your legs out horizontally.
Perform a sit-up and touch your toes (or as close as possible) Make sure your lower back
remains in contact with the mat.
EQUIPMENT:
Gym mat
PRIMARY MUSCLE:
Rectus abdominis
DEFAULT REPS:
15
